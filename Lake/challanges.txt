Mission 0: You should die: the fish should swim into land.
           This will terminate the connection to the lake.


Missions 1: Swim near land. 
     Land is near if "L" appear in the map sent by the server.
     Missions 1 and on are done in sequence: After completing a mission, the fish can attempt to solve next mission.
     The server sends the current attmpted mission.

Mission 2: swim through the "zero straits". 
     Near the coordintes zero, zero ther is an island. 
     You should pass between this island and the zero,zero corner of the lake.

Mission 3: swim next to anothr fish for 10 turns. 
     Swiming next to a fish means the other fish appears in your map
     Note: if a fish touches another fish, both die
